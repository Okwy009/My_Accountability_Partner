While Notion is our primary tool for systems, we can adapt our accountability frameworks to Trello, Asana, or simple spreadsheets if they serve your workflow better.

How much time does this require?

The goal is to save you time. Our weekly calls are 30-45 minutes, and daily check-ins take less than 2 minutes of your time.
